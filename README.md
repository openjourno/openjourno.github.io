# openJourno

## Read • Empower • Defy

**openJourno** is an **open-source, community-driven journalism platform** dedicated to **amplifying underrepresented voices** and delivering **trustworthy, transparent reporting**. We embrace **new wave journalism**—innovative, accountable, and engaging content for the modern reader.  

---

## Mission

**openJourno**, we believe in **truth, accessibility, and storytelling that matters**. We cover both global and local issues, providing readers with content that is **timely, factual, and insightful**, while celebrating **diverse perspectives** and innovative journalistic formats.  

Our mission is to empower journalists and readers alike:  

- **Journalists:** Share stories that matter without compromise.  
- **Readers:** Access credible reporting that informs and inspires.  
- **Community:** Foster collaboration and dialogue for a more open media ecosystem.  

---

## Social Media for Truth-Driven Journalism

**openJourno** uses social media as a **tool for responsible journalism**, not just promotion. Connect, share, and amplify stories that **hold power accountable** and **inform the public with integrity**.  

Follow us to discover, discuss, and contribute to journalism that **empowers, educates, and challenges the status quo**:  

- [Twitter](https://x.com/openJourno) – breaking news, updates, and insights  
- [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/openjourno) – visual storytelling and behind-the-scenes  
- [Threads](https://www.threads.net/@openjourno) – conversations on reporting, ethics, and ideas  
- [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/open-journo-96b4843a6/) – professional journalism network  
- [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@openJourno) – documentaries, interviews, and investigative features  

Your engagement fuels **journalism that is open, honest, and accountable**. Share responsibly, discuss critically, and amplify voices that deserve to be heard.

## Contributing

**openJourno** is **open-source** and thrives on community contributions. We welcome **journalists, writers, developers, and media enthusiasts** to help improve the platform or submit original content.  

**How to contribute:**  
1. Fork the repository  
2. Make your improvements or submit articles  
3. Open a pull request for review  

Your participation strengthens our commitment to **transparent, ethical journalism**.
